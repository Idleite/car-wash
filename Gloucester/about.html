About Us

car washing and detailing

Established in 1989, Patriots Car Wash is a family-owned facility situated in Gloucester City. Offering both a convenient drive-thru tunnel and self-serve options, our dedicated team of friendly staff is committed to providing every customer with a 5-star experience.

Our unique Unlimited Wash Program sets us apart, allowing customers to indulge in a sparkling clean car throughout the entire month for one low, budget-friendly price. At Patriots Car Wash, we pride ourselves on delivering top-notch service and a pristine shine that goes beyond expectations. Join us for unparalleled convenience, quality, and customer satisfaction.

  • Seats washing
  • Vacuum cleaning
  • Interior wet cleaning
  • Window wiping
