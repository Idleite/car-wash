Our state of the art 90 foot drive thru tunnel, uses soft cloth foam and premium quality soaps to ensure your vehicle paint is as clean as the day it drove off the lot

Elevate your car's interior with ease using 7 self-serve high-suction vacuums at Patriots Car Wash. Our state-of-the-art equipment ensures superior cleaning, making maintenance a breeze. Your satisfaction is our top priority in preserving your vehicle's impeccable condition.

Discover a diverse range of vending products at Patriots Car Wash, from air fresheners to towels. Plus, enjoy the convenience of on-site change machines for a seamless experience. Your satisfaction, our priority.
